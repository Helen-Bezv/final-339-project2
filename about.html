about Learn ©

We started out in 2018 as a fun project developed by our founders Helen Bezverhaia and Serena Ho. As the need for copywriting in the demanding product design industry grew, we found our site increasing in popularity- hence, why we've created a whole catalogue of exercises for you to practice copywriting. Learn C is a non-profit organization with a goal of providing accessible online and in-person training in brand copywriting for people aspiring to master their writing skills.
